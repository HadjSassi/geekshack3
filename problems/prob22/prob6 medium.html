• Business Planning

A businessman works X hours in a row during a day. He wants to plan profitable business during this day.
The problem is that in this day he can collect several deals, each one has a different meeting time and profitability than the other. While the businessman wants to save more money and time.
How can we help this businessman to plan his business?
We model this problem by the following input exemple:

    8
    4
    A 3 7
    B 5 4
    C 6 3
    D 4 3
The first line refers to X : Number of hours
The second line refers to profitable business Number
The rest of line represent a business meeting; it contains two values:
The first value is meeting duration and the second value is the profit afer meeting
Your Output must be:
    A B
    11
This means that businessman have to plan with "A" and "B" for profitable agreements, because "11" value is the best profit, in "8" hour of work.

NOTES

The businessman cannot exceed working time.

No spaces at the end of each output line.
