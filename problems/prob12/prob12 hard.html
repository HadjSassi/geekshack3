• Geeks way

Ahmed is one of the geeks club members. Given a list of integers, a starting point S and an ending point E,
can you help him find a way to go from S to E where the sum of the values of the cells is equal to a given score.

Input:
**Position of the starting point
**Position of the ending point
**The score
**The list of integers
Output:
**Valid or Not Valid

  • Example :
Input Output
[1,1]
[2,2]
7
[[1,2,0],[0,0,2],[1,1,3]] 		Valid
  • Notes
      You can't pass through the starting point and the ending point more than once.
      You can't pass through the other points (cells) more than 5 times.
