• Checkmate

Your friend Ahmed loves coding and chess. In order to enhance his skills, he wants to create a chess game. He needs your help to develop the functionnality responsable of deciding the "Checkmate".
The board will be modeled using 8x8 matrix and the index starts from 1.
He will give you the board state in order to decide if a checkmate will happen or not and the move to perform in order to capture the king.
He modeled the problem for you like this :

1 3
0 1
1 5 3
1 7 4 bishop
1 2 7 knight
0 4 7 rook
The first line indicates color and number of black pieces
The second line indicates color and number of white pieces
The third line indicates king color, its x-position, its y-position.
The other lines indicate the piece color, its x-position, its y-position, its type.
For the team type, 0 stands for the white piece and 1 stands for the black one.
Your output must be like this: 
0
This means that the king is safe
Another Example:
    1 2
    0 3
    1 1 3
    1 2 1 rook
    0 3 2 queen
    0 3 4 rook
    0 2 8 rook
In this case output must be like this: 
3
Here we have 4 possible moves to capture the king Notes: You have just one move to perform.

NOTES

You have just one move to perform.

No spaces in your Output !
