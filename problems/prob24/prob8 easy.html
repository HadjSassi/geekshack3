• Max Rectangle Area

you are asked to calculate the maximum area of a rectangle from the length given in input.
Indeed you are given as an example of input 

1 3 2 4
which represents the length of four arbitrary line segments.
The objective is to form with these segments a rectangle in its maximum area.
I.e. if we form a rectangle with these segments in the length of each arretes is 1 we will get 1m² as area, but if we adapt the segments to form the longer arretes we will get an area of 3m².

Here, we have 4 segements ; AB, BC, CD, EF .
Each semegnet has own length:
AB=1 
BC=3
CD=2
EF=4
In this case, we draw a rectangle with max area equals to 3m².
